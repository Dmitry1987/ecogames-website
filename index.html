Game based education

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world"
A saying by Nelson Mandela, Former South African president and renowned activist

We are an international team of game developers and 3D artists, committed to putting our skills to a common benefit of helping environmental activism organizations. We want to create awareness on critical environmental issues, through use of mobile games.

New approach to activism is necessary, because large corporations, businessmen, and politicians whos salary depends on ecologically destructive activity, usually consider activists annoying and their protests infuriating.

The protests can only postpone destructive activities, but not stop them. Activism organizations cannot win this war on their own.

But because the teens of today, are the businessmen and politicians of tomorrow, we can educate the next generation, using mobile game narrative and cinematic features. A thorough education on ecology and the environment, in a fun format of action and adventure games, will help our audience to make right decisions when voting for political parties in the nearest future.

Together with our supporters we at EcoGames can help shape the future for the better. Keeping our planet healthy, is a collective responsibility and the ultimate key is education. Your support of this project will be greatly appreciated!

Our current planned mobile games

Click image to see more (Work in progress)

"Arctic 30"

Sea adventures

A team of Greenpeace activists attempts a sail in three regions of the world, with a protest against environment endangering oil drilling and coal mining activities: Amazon reef, Great barrier reef, and the Arctic.

"Rescue team"

Arcade / Isometric

Mobile game designed for rising awareness among public about climate change implications like floods, hurricanes, forest fires, ice cap melting and rising sea levels.
The game will explain that if we don't reduce global warming, catastrophic consequences await next generations.

Orangutan escape

3D Runner

The game will highlight the problem of deforestation, by telling a story of Orangutan monkeys family fleeing from forest fire. And after the escape from fire was successfull, they find out their home jungle was devastated from the other side, by Palm Oil corporation, and they have nowhere to go.
A dramatic story for an action runner game.

Tsunami Runner

2D side-scrolling action

An action game about the danger of increasing seismic activity and tsunami risks, the player will control a character trying to escape a tsunami wave, and get to safety, in a fast action packed platformer runner game.
